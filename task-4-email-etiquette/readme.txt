## 🛠️ Task 1: Essential Digital Toolkit

Understanding, evaluating, and creating information using digital tools is the foundation for navigating the online world effectively and ethically. Below is a quick reference guide to essential digital practices.

### 📈 Useful Digital Tools
Boost your productivity and research capabilities:
* **Notion:** An all-in-one workspace essential for note-taking, task management, and organization.
* **Mendeley:** A powerful reference management tool crucial for academic research and professional success.

### 🔒 Safe Internet Practices
Protect yourself and your data online:
* **Enable 2FA:** Always use Two-Factor Authentication to secure your accounts.
* **Strong Passwords:** Use complex, unique passwords for different platforms.
* **Phishing Awareness:** Remain vigilant against suspicious emails and links to safeguard your personal data.

### 🌐 Professional Online Presence
Build and maintain your digital brand:
* **LinkedIn:** Maintain a polished and up-to-date profile.
* **Networking:** Actively use professional platforms to connect with industry peers and showcase your skills, projects, and achievements.

### ✉️ General Email Etiquette
Communicate professionally in all digital correspondence:
* **Subject Lines:** Use clear, concise, and descriptive subject lines.
* **Greetings:** Always start with an appropriate formal greeting.
* **Signatures:** Include a professional signature with your full name, title, and contact information.

## 📝 Task 4: Professional Email Drafts

Below are standard templates for common professional scenarios, including academic requests and corporate inquiries.

### 1. Academic Extension Request
**Purpose:** To respectfully request a deadline extension due to unforeseen circumstances.

```text
Subject: Extension Request: [Assignment 05: Digital Logic Design] – Rudra Pandey

Dear Professor Baral,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to respectfully request a brief extension on the [Assignment 05: Digital Logic Design] due on 12/03/2026. Unfortunately, I am currently out of the college hostel on leave to attend a family function, which has set back my progress.

I have already completed a significant portion of the work and would appreciate the opportunity to submit a high-quality final version. Would it be possible to submit the assignment by 15/03/2026 instead?

I understand if this is not possible and am happy to provide any necessary documentation regarding my absence if required.

Thank you for your time and for considering my request.

Best regards,

Rudra Pandey
